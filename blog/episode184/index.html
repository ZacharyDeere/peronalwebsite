Going Merry

My One Piece Blog:

*SPOILER WARNING*

Episode 184:

Luffy Falls! Eneru's Judgment and Nami's Wish!

Summary
 Picking up from the end of the last episode, the punch thrown by Luffy that was cut short ended up connecting sending Eneru flying across the Maxim. From the looks of it all you can see is the whites of Eneru’s eyes, which would mean that Luffy knocked him out. However, seconds later Eneru managed to get on his knees and was spitting blood. Struggling to stand Eneru continued to announce that he is unbeatable and the almighty god. He reassured Luffy that he was going to defeat him and make him feel ashamed.

At this point Maxim finally gathered up enough momentum to lift into the sky. The townspeople of Angel Island were already evacuating, but seeing the ship in the sky with the thunderclouds bellowing out made them hurry much faster.

Back aboard the ship Eneru blocks one of Luffy’s punches by melting a ball of gold off of the ship and forcing it to dry around Luffy’s arm leaving him weighed down. While Luffy was helpless Eneru rolls the gold ball off of the ship thinking that the weight of the ball would immediately send him flying toward the ground. Much to Eneru’s surprise Luffy is able to brace himself by holding onto the ship’s railing. This does not last long though, Eneru destroys the railing causing Luffy to fall.

Aisa and Pierre (Gan Fall’s mythical pet) notice Luffy falling and fly under the ship in an attempt to catch him. However, Eneru notices them and sends a huge lightning bolt in their direction. Eneru assumes that the bolt killed all three of the individuals and continues on. At first Eneru’s destination was for the edge of the island, but he changed his course after remembering the golden bell that Robin said was hidden on the island. At this point Nami was fulling rejecting Eneru’s ideas and openly told Eneru that he is dumb for wanting treasure and not friendships. Eneru became frustrated with Nami and decided that he is going to kill her.

Meanwhile back on the Going Merry, the Strawhat's Ship, Usopp and Sanji finally wake up after being unconscious and ill for the past few days from a fight with Eneru. Sanji and Usopp spot Nami alone with Eneu amongst the Maxim and seek out to rescue her. Using Usopp’s gadget called the “Usopp AAaaaAAAaaaAA,” Usopp and Sanji manage to grapple hook the Maxim as it passed over head. The episode ends with Eneru about to attack Nami while Usopp and Sanji climb up the rope to board the Maxim.

My Thoughts
 I truly believe that Usopp and Sanji will make it in time to protect Nami. Although, I do not think that the duo can defeat Eneru. I believe that in the next episode while Usopp and Sanji are attempting to take on Eneru, Pierre will appear in the distance with Aisa and Luffy on his back. Thus, Luffy will step back in and try his luck back in battle. I think this because Luffy has been the only opponent besides Wyper to successfully hurt Eneru. On the other hand, I would love for Usopp and Sanji to prove me wrong. Usopp is known for his crazy gadgets and seeing him pull one out of his pouch to take down Eneru would not be much of a surprise.

The Skypiea ark has felt very drawn out and lacking in substance. I hate to say this but I am getting tired of being in the sky and am ready for our beloved Straw Hat crew to be back in the ocean. Seeing Eneru finally defeated and the crew returning to the blue sea will be a relieving moment.
